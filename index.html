Meet the founder

The Explorer Behind the Tree

I'm Rome Pearson — born in Brooklyn, New York, raised in Brooklyn and Mattapan, Massachusetts, and now planting roots in Tyler, Texas. Over the past 30+ years I've led restaurant teams, operated satellite networks for a global corporation, trained hundreds of employees, and graduated as class valedictorian. I've always found a way to grow wherever I was planted.

"It started as a search for a niche and a way to generate income remotely. It became a calling."

Last year, I saw an opportunity to do something I'd never done before — build something entirely my own, from home, on my own terms. That's how PearTree Explorers Tutoring was born. I wanted to create a warm, joyful, and accessible space where children could discover the magic of stories, numbers, and history — delivered via Zoom and available in both English and Spanish.

I'm Rome — and this is my greatest adventure yet.

Meet our mascot

Say Hello to Lil' Romey!

Every great adventure needs a great guide — and ours is Lil' Romey, our animated spokesperson and the friendly face of PearTree Explorers Tutoring.

Dressed in his signature tan suit and green tie, Lil' Romey leads young explorers through reading adventures, math discoveries, and history journeys in a way that feels like play — because learning should always feel that way.

Whether on YouTube, in a Zoom session, or across our social channels, Lil' Romey is here to greet every child with a smile and say: "Let's explore!"
